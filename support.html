常见问题

FAQ

按住右 ⌘ 没有反应？

Nothing happens when I hold right ⌘?

请确认已授予麦克风和辅助功能权限。前往「系统设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 辅助功能」，确保 EdgeTalk 已勾选。

Make sure microphone and accessibility permissions are granted. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility, ensure EdgeTalk is checked.

识别结果不准确？

Recognition accuracy is low?

在设置中确认主要语言与实际说话语言一致。如需更高准确率，可选择"增强"校正模型（需下载约 2.4GB）。

Confirm the primary language in Settings matches what you're speaking. For higher accuracy, choose the "Enhanced" correction model (~2.4GB download).

需要网络连接吗？

Does it require internet?

不需要。EdgeTalk 完全离线运行，所有识别在本地完成。

No. EdgeTalk runs completely offline. All recognition happens locally.

支持哪些 Mac？

Which Macs are supported?

macOS 14.0 或更高版本，需配备 Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4)。

macOS 14.0 or later. Requires Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4).

为什么需要辅助功能权限？

Why does EdgeTalk require Accessibility?

EdgeTalk 以无障碍为设计核心。该权限让不便于使用键盘的用户通过说话打字——按住右 Cmd 说话，松手自动粘贴到光标位置。不会读取其他应用的内容。

EdgeTalk is built for accessibility. This permission enables voice typing for those who can't easily use a keyboard — hold right Cmd, speak, release to paste at cursor. Never reads content from other apps.
