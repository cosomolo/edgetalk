FAQ

Q: Nothing happens when I hold right ⌘?

Make sure microphone and accessibility permissions are granted. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility, and ensure EdgeTalk is checked.

Q: Recognition accuracy is low?

Make sure your preferred language in Settings matches the language you're speaking. For higher accuracy, choose the "Enhanced" correction model (~2.4GB download).

Q: Does it require an internet connection?

No. EdgeTalk runs completely offline. All recognition happens locally on your device.

Q: Which Macs are supported?

macOS 14.0 or later. Requires Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4).

Q: Why does EdgeTalk require Accessibility permission?

EdgeTalk uses Accessibility to paste recognized text at your cursor position. This permission also enables voice typing for users with motor impairments or typing difficulties — it's a core part of making text input accessible to everyone. EdgeTalk never reads, collects, or accesses content from other apps.
